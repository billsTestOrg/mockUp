Getting Started

Follow these steps to get set up, then check out the event ideas below.

  • Step 1:

    Set Up a Website

    Follow these instructions to set up a free website, message board and chat room for your study group. Stuck? Ask for help.

  • Step 2:

    Get On The Map

    Get in touch with the Mozilla Science Lab on Twitter, the forum, or by email to let us know you're starting a study group, and we'll put you on the map & let everyone know.

  • Step 3:

    Get the Word Out

    Check out our guide to letting everyone know about your new study group.

  • Step 4:

    Throw an Event!

    You're all set up - time to meet new friends & colleagues and work together. Check out our event ideas & how-tos below.
